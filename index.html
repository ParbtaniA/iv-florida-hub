How to use this hub

Select a category from the left sidebar or the tiles above to view all five Florida centers. Click any center card to open a document drawer — browse subfolders and files without leaving the page. All files open directly in Google Drive.

Documents are organized and maintained by the IV Florida regional team. To upload or organize new files, use the _Dump folder in Google Drive and contact your regional coordinator.
